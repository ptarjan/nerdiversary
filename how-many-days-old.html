How many days old are you?

Enter your birthday and get your age in days — plus weeks, hours, minutes, and live-ticking seconds — instantly. Counting in days makes age more interesting: a birthday only comes once a year, but a round thousand days comes around every 2¾ years.

The math

  • Days old = days elapsed since your birth date (this calculator counts calendar days from midnight)
  • An 80-year life is about 29,200 days — or 4,170 weeks, or 2.5 billion seconds
  • The famous ones: 10,000 days ≈ 27.4 years, 20,000 days ≈ 54.8 years, 30,000 days ≈ 82.1 years

Know your day count and you unlock the good milestones: day 12,345, day 11,111, day 16,384 (2¹⁴), and every palindrome in between.
