When is your half birthday?

Your half birthday is the day you turn exactly N½ years old — celebrated by summer babies with winter parties, and by anyone who thinks one birthday a year is not enough. This calculator finds your half, quarter, and three-quarter birthdays for every year of your life.

The math

  • A half year here is half of 365.2425 days: 182.62 days — not just “same day, six months later”
  • That is why your true half birthday can drift a day from the naive date
  • You also get ¼ (91.3 days) and ¾ (273.9 days) birthdays for extra celebrations

Half birthdays are the gateway drug to nerdy milestones. Once you celebrate 29½, you are ready for 1 billion seconds.
