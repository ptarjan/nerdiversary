When will you (or your kid) be 1 million minutes old?

One million minutes arrives 1 year and 328 days after birth — a perfect nerdy milestone for toddlers, and a favorite of spreadsheet-inclined parents. Enter a birthday (birth time recommended — minutes matter here) to find the exact moment.

The math

  • 1,000,000 minutes ÷ 1,440 minutes per day ≈ 694.4 days
  • 694.4 ÷ 365.2425 ≈ 1.90 years — a few weeks before the second birthday
  • The million-second birthday comes much sooner: 11.6 days old

Broadway did the math first: 525,600 minutes is one year. A million minutes is one year, ten months, and a lot more diapers.
