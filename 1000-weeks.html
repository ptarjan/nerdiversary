When will you be 1,000 weeks old?

Your 1,000th week arrives just past your 19th birthday — 7,000 days into your life, before your brain has even finished wiring itself. Enter a birthday to find this and every other week-count milestone.

The math

  • 1,000 weeks × 7 = 7,000 days
  • 7,000 ÷ 365.2425 ≈ 19.16 years — about 2 months after turning 19
  • 2,000 weeks lands at ~38.3 years; 3,000 weeks at ~57.5; 4,000 weeks is a stretch goal

The average human life is about 4,000 weeks — the number Oliver Burkeman built a whole book around. Knowing which week you are on is clarifying.
